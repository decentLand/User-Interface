About Tribus

A Tribus is a wrapper around an Arweave DAO, which is known for Profit Sharing Community or PSC.

The Tribus extends the DAO functionalities by creating and adding a social experience for it. By aggregating FinTech with Governance Policy, the Tribus can potentially re-enforce the its DAO's tokenomics.

Only the PSC create is allowed to create and update the DAO's Tribus
