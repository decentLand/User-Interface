What is a Tribus?

the Tribus is a new kind of social network communities, it's an "chat over PSC" . That is, a PSC or Profit Sharing Community is like the DAO on Ethereum. A Tribus is like a community owned subreddit The admin have no power over the community members, unless they unite on one decision -- democracy


The Tribus is based on the Community Ownership Economy model.
